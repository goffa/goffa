From the Artist

August 1, 2006

Amora by David Lanham

This is a large set of icons that I started about a year and a half ago. I quite like music videos, especially ones by Bjork, and even more so videos by Chris Cunningham. So when I saw the video for ‘All is Full of Love’, I was very taken by it.

I’ve also always like the whole black and white robot motif, so it’s couldn’t be helped but to make a set of icons with all that material as a jumping off point. For the most part, I’ve taken my own direction with the icons, but many of them are heavily inspired by the music video, but the best way to learn is by watching the masters :)

Included in the set is a full system replacement as well as many applications, I’m sure I’ve missed a lot of them, but there’s always time for add-ons!

I hope that you enjoy the set!
